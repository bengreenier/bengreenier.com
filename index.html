Ben Greenier

My name is Ben, and I’m a software engineer at Microsoft. I’m obsessed with game development, and game development technologies, though my background is in backend service development. You can find me streaming development on Twitch, sharing thoughts on Twitter, and read my medium blog as well.

At work, I tend to focus on creating opensource tools that help make developer’s lives easier. My team and I do this by partnering with other engineering companies, learning from their problems, and building solutions together. Then my team goes out and shares what we built, how we built it, and what we learned with other cool folks that are interested.

Outside of work, I focus on creating games, game frameworks, and streamer tools. This means I’m often creating content for Unity, Unreal, the web, or the desktop. You’ll see a lot of c++, c#, and javascript from me on GitHub.
